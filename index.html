Welcome to George Hotz fan page!

George Hotz is a legendary hacker, programmer, entrepreneur, thinker; and overall a very fun person to know about and to listen to.

Claim To Fame

First to jailbreak an iPhone in 2007.

Hacked PlayStation 3 in 2010 which led to discovery of security vulnerabilities. A lawsuit by Sony followed.

Started Comma AI - a company developing self-driving car technology.

Trying to commoditize the petaflop through Tiny Corp - a company that manufactured computers.
